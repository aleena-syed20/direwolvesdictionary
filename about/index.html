Hello.

Are you still twiddling your thumbs waiting for The Winds of Winter to come out? Are you over occasional updates by Martin once again letting readers know the book is in progress?

Well, I don't really know what else to do in the meantime instead of making a compilation of theories that fans have had time to develop and explore with the decade long hiatus of the series.

Ranging from whether certain characters are actually dead, alive, and everything in between, I hope you enjoy reading Direwolves' Dictionary.

Aleena Syed, 2020
